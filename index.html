About Me

Short Bio

I work at the intersection of health data science, multimodal learning, and intelligent sensing. My recent research explores how wearable sensors, 3D skeletal motion data, and interpretable machine learning methods can support fall risk prediction, rehabilitation evaluation, and neurological disorder monitoring.

I have published first-author and co-authored papers in journals including IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, BMC Geriatrics, and BMC Public Health.
